Feedoo is a feed reader that would be your everyday coffee companion. With a multi-themed interface, it would give you the ability to choose the top news feeds out there based on your interests, language preferences, sports interests. A later phase would allow for news feeds to be pushed to facebook, twitter and evernote accounts as well.

It also integrates with dropbox and allows your to news links that you have found interesting and wish to revisit. It allows for the first 30 feeds to be free and additional feeds have a tiered charging system. (i.e. the next 20 feeds would be an additional $2 per month). Since Feedoo is web based, you can login to the portal from almost anywhere and through any device. It saves the state of your articles read/unread and you can resume reading news from where your left off. Cool right ?

FAQ

Can I signup for a free account on Feedoo?
Yes. You can try the no-ads version of Feedoo for upto 15 feeds. The addition of additional feeds would enable ads on the software. Ads would get disabled on purchase of the basic or higher version of Feedoo.
How can I disable the ads?
To disable the ads, you could buy the basic subscription of $2 per month which would give you subscribe to 50 'Ad free' feeds per month.
Can I import my subscriptions from google reader ?
This is something that is being worked on and would be available in the next release.
